📚 Why we use 7% as the investment return

The 7% real return (after inflation) is the most widely used assumption in financial independence planning — grounded in over a century of market data.

Shiller dataset
150+ years of US equity returns
~7% real
MSCI World Index
Long-run global equity average
~7% real
Trinity Study (1998)
Academic basis for the 4% rule
7% assumed
Vanguard Australia
Long-run Australian equity research
~7–8% real

Past returns don't guarantee future results. FirePath uses 7% as a planning assumption, not a prediction.
