Questions

Honest answers

Is FirePath financial advice? ↓
No — FirePath is an educational tool. We show you what different numbers and scenarios mean based on historical data and your inputs. We never tell you what to do with your money. Always consider your personal circumstances and speak to a licensed financial advisor for personalised advice.
How does the 7% growth rate work? ↓
7% is the long-run historical real return (after inflation) for diversified stock market investments — derived from over a century of global market data. It's the standard rate used across the FIRE community and is the basis of the 4% safe withdrawal rule. It's not guaranteed, but it's the most evidence-based assumption available for long-term planning.
What is a freedom number? ↓
Your freedom number is 25x your annual spending — the amount you need invested so that withdrawing 4% per year covers your lifestyle indefinitely. Based on the Trinity Study, this approach has historically sustained portfolios over 30+ year periods. Once you hit your freedom number, work becomes optional.
Is my data private? ↓
Yes. Your numbers are used only to calculate your results and generate your AI insight. We don't store your financial data or share it with third parties. The AI processes your input to generate a response and nothing is retained.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Yes — cancel anytime, no questions asked. We're confident FirePath Pro will be worth far more than $6/month to you, but we never want you to feel locked in.
