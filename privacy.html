Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FirePath is built by an individual developer based in Australia. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We've written it in plain English because we think you deserve to actually understand it.

What we collect

When you use FirePath, we may collect:

  • Account information — your email address when you sign up or log in.
  • Financial inputs — the numbers you enter into the calculator (income, savings, super balance, age, debt). These are used only to generate your results and are not verified, audited, or shared.
  • Usage data — which features you use, your persona selection, pay cycle, and general behaviour in the app. This helps us understand what's working and what isn't.
  • Feedback — if you submit feedback through the feedback form, we collect your responses and optionally your email.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or any government identifiers.
  • We do not collect payment card details — payments are handled securely by Stripe.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to third parties. Ever.

How we use your data

  • To generate your personalised FirePath results and AI insights.
  • To save your inputs so you don't have to re-enter them on your next visit.
  • To send you account-related emails (sign-up confirmation, password reset).
  • To improve the product based on how people use it.

We do not use your financial inputs to make decisions about you, profile you for advertising, or share them with any third party.

Who we share data with

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to run FirePath:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure.
  • Stripe — payment processing for FirePath Pro subscriptions. We never see or store your card details.
  • Anthropic — the AI that powers FirePath's personalised insights. Your financial inputs are sent to Anthropic's API to generate your insight and are not used to train their models.
  • Resend — email delivery for account and notification emails.

No other third parties have access to your data.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We use row-level security so your data is only accessible to your own account. We take reasonable steps to protect your information but no system is 100% secure — please use a strong password.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. We'll action this within 30 days. You can also update or correct your information by logging in to your account.

Cookies and local storage

FirePath uses browser local storage to remember your session and preferences (such as your persona selection and commitment tracker). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Learning Lab data

If you are a Pro user, FirePath saves your Learning Lab activity to your account — including which articles you have read and any articles you have saved. This data is:

  • Stored securely in Supabase against your account only
  • Used only to personalise your learning experience and sync it across devices
  • Never sold, shared, or used for any purpose outside of FirePath
  • Deletable on request — email us at hello@firepath.pro

Free users' Learning Lab activity is stored only in their browser's local storage and is not sent to our servers.

Children

FirePath is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of FirePath after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@firepath.pro
