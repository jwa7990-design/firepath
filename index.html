"I'm a support worker. I built FirePath because nobody ever explained this stuff to me in plain English — and I wish they had."

Over the years I've had conversations with lots of different people about life, about stress, about making ends meet. One thing keeps coming up: most people don't really understand how money works. Not because they're not smart. Because nobody ever explained it.

My wife and I decided to take some calculated risks to try and move our family forward. Those risks felt uncomfortable at the time.
