What is FIRE?

Four letters that change how you see your working life

FIRE isn't about being rich or retiring at 30. It's about understanding that financial independence is achievable for everyday people — and knowing exactly where you stand.

F
Financial
Building enough wealth that money stops being a daily source of stress
I
Independence
The freedom to choose — work because you want to, not because you have to
R
Retire
Not necessarily stop working — but having the option, on your terms
E
Early
Earlier than you think. Much earlier than the system tells you

Why I built this platform
"I hope it helps your family the way understanding this stuff helped mine."

I'm a support worker. Over the years I've had conversations with lots of different people — young and old — about life, about stress, about making ends meet. And one thing keeps coming up: most people don't really understand how money works. Not because they're not smart. Because nobody ever explained it in plain English, and it's not something school ever taught me either.

I'm no expert. But over the last few years my wife and I decided to take some calculated risks to try and move our family forward. Those risks felt uncomfortable at the time, but if we hadn't made that choice we wouldn't have achieved what we have today. Some of our choices were lucky calls — not always down to research and knowing everything. But what I will say is I wish we'd made them a decade earlier, because as you'll learn, the more time you have the easier it is to get ahead.

When you place your money somewhere different to the banks you have to learn about it. And as we learnt, we started to understand things the banks don't explain and the finance industry makes deliberately complicated or feel scary.

I wanted to build something that shows people what's actually possible based on their situation. Not a sales pitch. Not a financial advisor. Just your numbers, your timeline, and the knowledge to understand what it all means and take your next step with confidence. We live in a world where AI can give you amazing insights at your fingertips — in FirePath it's all designed to trigger thinking and learning, and give you tools in a way you'll understand no matter what level of knowledge you have.

FirePath has real costs to run — AI, servers, infrastructure — but the plan is to keep it as affordable as possible. $6 a month felt right. Less than a coffee. No lock-in. Because I built this for people who are watching every dollar, not people who aren't.

— Jay Founder, FirePath 🇦🇺
