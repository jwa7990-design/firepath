What is FIRE?

Four letters that change how you see your working life

FIRE isn't about being rich. It isn't about retiring at 30. It's about understanding that financial independence is achievable — and knowing exactly how to get there.

F
Financial
Building enough wealth that money is no longer a constraint on how you live
I
Independence
The freedom to choose — work because you want to, not because you have to
R
Retire
Not necessarily stop working — but having the option to, on your terms, at any age
E
Early
Earlier than you think. Much earlier than the system tells you
