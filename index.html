How it works

Three things FirePath does differently.

It shows you. It educates you. It empowers you.

1
Shows you where you actually stand
Most of us have a rough idea of our finances. But rough ideas don't give you a plan. FirePath turns your income and savings into a real date — the day you could choose to stop.

And if that date doesn't fit your life? FirePath helps you understand what you can do about it — in plain English, no judgment.
2
Tells you what actually moves the needle
Not generic tips. Your numbers. What happens to your date if you save $50 more a week? If your money was invested instead of sitting in a bank? FirePath shows you the exact difference — in years.

The Learning Lab reads your inputs and recommends education built around your situation — so you can figure out what to do next and why it matters.
3
Sits beside you as your situation changes
Life changes. Your numbers should too. Every time you come back, FirePath updates your picture. Over time you'll watch the most important number in your life get smaller — and that feeling is what we want you to experience.

Over the years, I've chatted with heaps of people about life, stress and how to make ends meet. One thing that always pops up is that most people don't quite get how money works. It's not because they're not smart — it's just that nobody ever explained it to them.

My wife and I decided to take some calculated risks to help our family move forward. Those risks felt a bit uncomfortable at the time.
