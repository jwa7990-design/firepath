🦘 How super fits your freedom plan
Most Australians have two separate financial timelines running at the same time — and most calculators ignore one of them.

Timeline 1 — Your freedom savings. Everything outside super. ETFs, cash, offset, shares. You can access this any time. This is what FirePath uses to calculate your freedom date.

Timeline 2 — Your super. Compounding quietly in the background, with employer contributions going in every pay cycle. Locked until age 60, but growing the whole time.

The goal isn't just to hit your freedom number with savings alone — it's to understand how these two timelines connect, and what gap (if any) you actually need to close yourself.

Super projected at 7% annual growth with employer contributions after 15% contributions tax. Withdrawals after age 60 are generally tax-free. Educational only — not financial advice.
